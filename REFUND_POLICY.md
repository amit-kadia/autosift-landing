# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** July 1, 2026

Thank you for choosing AutoSift.

### 1. Subscriptions and Payments
- AutoSift is a B2B productivity tool sold on a subscription basis.
- We offer a free trial to evaluate the software before purchasing.

### 2. Refund Policy
- Subscription fees are generally non-refundable once the subscription is activated.
- If you face technical issues that prevent you from using the tool, and our support team cannot resolve the issue within 7 business days, a refund may be initiated.

### 3. Timeline for Refunds
- Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 7 working days.
- The refunded amount will be credited back to your original payment method.

### 4. Cancellation
- You can cancel your subscription at any time.
- Cancellation will stop the next billing cycle. No partial refunds are provided for the remaining subscription period.

### 5. Contact Us
- For support or refund queries, contact us at **autosiftindia+support@gmail.com**.
