❓ Frequently Asked Questions ▶
How does the 15-day free trial work? +
Every new user gets a one-time 15-day trial of the PRO features when they sign in with Google inside the Chrome Extension. No credit card is required to claim the trial. Once the trial ends, you will revert to the FREE Forever plan automatically unless you choose to subscribe.
Is my payment secure? +
Yes, all payments are securely processed via Razorpay, India's leading payment gateway. We support all major Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), and Netbanking. Your credentials are fully encrypted and never stored on our servers.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from the settings page. Once canceled, your PRO features will remain active until the end of your billing cycle, and no further charges will be made.
Do I get a refund if I cancel? +
Please refer to our Refund Policy for detailed terms. We offer full/partial refunds under specific conditions if requested within our refund request window. Contact support via email or WhatsApp if you need help.
